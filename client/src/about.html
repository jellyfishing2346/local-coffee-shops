About Local Coffee Shops Guide

This web app helps you discover the best local coffee shops in the United States. Browse, search, filter, and explore shop details, reviews, and locations on the map. Submit your own reviews and help others find their next favorite spot!

  • Built with Express, vanilla HTML/CSS/JS, and Picocss
  • Features animated cards, search/filter, sorting, pagination, and map integration
  • Accessible and mobile-friendly design
  • Open source and ready for your contributions!
